# Phase 4: Advanced Features

## Objective
Implement Fatigue Risk Management, Training Tracking, and Notifications System

## Timeline
4-6 hours

---

## Module 1: Fatigue Risk Management System (FRMS)

### Features
- Boeing Alertness Model (BAM) implementation
- Circadian rhythm analysis
- Sleep opportunity calculation
- Fatigue score prediction
- Alertness level monitoring
- Fatigue risk alerts

### Deliverables
- Fatigue calculation engine
- API endpoints for fatigue monitoring
- Crew fatigue dashboard
- Fatigue reports and analytics

---

## Module 2: Training & Qualifications Tracking

### Features
- Track crew qualifications (aircraft types, ratings)
- Monitor license expiry dates
- Track medical certificate validity
- Training records and completion
- Recurrent training scheduling
- Qualification alerts (30/60/90 days before expiry)

### Deliverables
- Training management system
- Qualification tracking
- Expiry alerts
- Training calendar
- Crew qualification reports

---

## Module 3: Notifications & Messaging System

### Features
- Real-time notifications for crew
- Email notifications
- SMS notifications (optional)
- Push notifications
- Notification preferences
- Message templates
- Notification history

### Notification Types
- Roster published
- Bid period opened/closed
- Bid awarded/denied
- Trade request received/approved
- Leave request approved/denied
- Training due
- License/medical expiring
- Schedule changes
- Emergency callouts

### Deliverables
- Notification engine
- API endpoints
- Notification preferences UI
- Notification center
- Email templates

---

## Success Criteria

- Fatigue scores calculated accurately
- Training expiry alerts sent 30/60/90 days in advance
- Notifications delivered within 1 minute
- Email delivery rate > 95%
- All crew receive critical notifications
- Notification preferences respected

---

## Implementation Priority

1. **High Priority:**
   - Fatigue monitoring (safety critical)
   - License/medical expiry alerts (compliance critical)
   - Roster change notifications (operational critical)

2. **Medium Priority:**
   - Training tracking
   - General notifications
   - Notification preferences

3. **Low Priority:**
   - SMS notifications
   - Advanced fatigue analytics
   - Custom notification templates
